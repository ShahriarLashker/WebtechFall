Published: November 10, 2025

The International Olympic Committee has officially announced Mumbai, India as the host city for the 2032 Summer Olympics. The decision marks the first time India will host the Summer Games, promising to showcase the country's rich culture and sporting passion on the world stage. Plans include state-of-the-art facilities, sustainable infrastructure, and programs to promote sports development across the nation.
